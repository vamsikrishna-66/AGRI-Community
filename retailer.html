The RETAILER

Retailers are responsible for connecting farmers and ensuring that the products reach the end-users in a proper manner. They act as a medium between farmers and consumers. They are like an accessible channel for consumers in purchasing goods and services by making it easier for consumers to shop and find what they need at their convenience. Retailers are also prime contributors to the economy by providing employment oppurtunities and driving economic growth in the local community. By creating jobs and generating revenue, retailers help to stimulate economic activity and support the development of vibrant and thriving communities.
