VISION

For many years, famers in India have very less choice in selecting their market or buyers of their crops. In most states, selling of farm products should be done through that particular state's mandis. So usually middlemen exploit farmers and keep most of the profit to themselves. Through our web app we aim to help farmers by giving them a choice to select their buyers/markets at their own will or sell their produce directly to customer. For a buyer, it gives information about available crops. There are options to check if they crops are fresh are not. They can also contact the farmers, at his phone number which will be made available on the app. Consumers can buy from the retailer or famer, check if crops are fresh and have options to check quality of crops.

about us

Welcome to our web application, a platform designed to help farmers and r etailers connect directly, without the need for middlemen. Our team is passionate about empowering farmers and improving the efficiency and transparency of the food supply chain. We understand the challenges that farmers and retailers face in the traditional food supply chain, from high transaction costs to lack of transparency and control. That's why we've created a platform that connects farmers and retailers directly, cutting out the middlemen and improving the efficiency and transparency of the supply chain. Our web application provides farmers with a platform to showcase their products and connect with retailers who are looking for fresh, high-quality produce. Retailers, on the other hand, can access a wide range of locally sourced produce from farmers across the region, and can easily place orders and manage their inventory through our platform. Our team consists of experienced professionals who have extensive knowledge in both technology and agriculture. We're dedicated to creating a platform that's both user-friendly and effective, and that helps farmers and retailers build more direct and mutually beneficial relationships. We believe that by connecting farmers and retailers directly, we can improve the sustainability of the food supply chain, reduce food waste, and increase the availability of fresh and locally sourced produce. Contact us today to learn more about our web application and how it can benefit your farming or retail operations. We look forward to hearing from you!
